Chapter Three

Three Worlds.
Zero Overlap.

Three stages. Three windows of the day. Three sonic identities. They never compete — they pass the crowd from one to the next, each complete in itself.

Main Stage · Full Capacity · 7pm – 6am · All Four Nights
ZUNGU MAIN
Built into the island. The dominant, singular experience. Total theatrical commitment.

South-facing. Sound travels out to sea, away from Port Antonio. The stage does not feel built — it feels grown. Tropical forest frames every sightline. Lush canopy overhead. The Caribbean visible beyond the crowd.

Full production at Tomorrowland scale for the space — LED walls, lighting rig, laser, water feature, pyro. But the production serves the environment. The island is the set design. Technology amplifies it.

One concept per night. One headliner per night. When Zungu Main is active, nothing else competes. From 7pm until the last act closes, this is the only stage that matters.

Chapter Four

Four Nights.
One Argument.

Each night is a chapter. The root. The spread. The return. The origin. Jamaica's DNA — exported, mutated, and brought home.

01
Saturday · 19 June 2027The Root
THE ROOT

Jamaica's percussion DNA to global house. Where it begins. The first night anchors the argument.

Night 1 belongs to the argument itself. Tribal house. Deep, hypnotic, rooted in percussion lineage that Jamaica planted in global music without ever getting credited for it. Bontan headlines. The crowd arrives not yet knowing what this festival is. Night 1 tells them.

Origins opens at 6am with a local Portland Parish selector. The island speaks first. Rebirth opens at 4pm. The crowd walks the forest path at 7:45pm. Bontan plays 11pm–2am minimum.

Production concept: natural materials, organic forms. The stage technology is present but deferential. The forest is the dominant visual.

THE SPREAD

Global south mutation. Brazilian Baile, Afrotech, Latin house. The argument leaves the island and comes back changed.

Night 2 follows the music outward. Jamaica exported its DNA to Brazil, to Lagos, to London. Night 2 plays back what those cities did with it. Afrotech, Baile Funk influences, Latin house, global south mutation.

Origins plays a harder jungle set morning. Rebirth leans into Afrotech at sunset. Night 2 headliner TBC — Vintage Culture, Themba, or a curated concept night.

03
Monday · 21 June 2027The Return
THE RETURN

Afro-house. Africa back to the Caribbean. The DNA completes its journey home. Black Coffee closes. Peak night.

Night 3 is the peak. The argument resolves. African house — from South Africa specifically — is the clearest example of Jamaica's exported DNA mutating, evolving, and returning home. Black Coffee is the closing statement of that argument, delivered at full production on the island that started it.

Shimza opens for Black Coffee. The crowd has been through two nights. They know the handoff, the forest path, the ritual. Night 3 converts all of that accumulated experience into something close to catharsis.

Full production. Full 5am close. The longest night of the festival.

Tuesday · 22 June 2027The Origin
THE ORIGIN

Jungle, roots, dub — pure Jamaican. No international headline. The island closes how it opened: in its own voice.

Night 4 has no international headline. By design. After three nights of following the DNA across the globe and watching it return, the final night returns to source. Pure Jamaican electronic: Equiknoxx, Kode9 b2b Shy FX, local Portland Parish selectors.

This is the night that makes RA write the feature. The argument stated plainly, without intermediary, on the island that earned the right to state it. Night 4 is not the biggest night. It is the most important one.

The Hard Rules
Origins and Rebirth never run simultaneously with each other or with Zungu Main.
When Black Coffee is playing, no other stage exists. The Pier opens midnight only — after the headline has the crowd.
The forest path between Rebirth and Zungu Main is fully lit and staffed every evening. The walk is part of the experience.
All activity zones close at 3:30pm. Rebirth opens 4pm. The transition is managed, not abrupt.
The Pier runs midnight to sunrise at 200 cap, by discovery only. No wristband upgrade. No announcement. You find it or you don't.
MainLead production company. Full LED, lighting, sound, pyro. Generator redundancy ×2.
OriginsSecond company. Sound quality first. Warm amber lighting only. Sunrise does the visual work.
RebirthThird company. Mid-scale. Dusk-calibrated warm lighting. Manages forest path during handoff.
The PierFourth company. Minimal. 200 cap. Sound and basic lighting only. The sea is the production.
